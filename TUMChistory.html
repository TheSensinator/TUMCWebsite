History of the Church

Tonica United Methodist Church is a welcoming community dedicated to sharing the love of Christ with all who seek spiritual growth. Our church is committed to supporting each person on their faith journey and helping them connect with others in the community.

Founded in 1904, our church has been a cornerstone in the Tonica community from the very beginning.

Whether you are new to the faith or looking for a new church home, we invite you to join us as we walk together in faith and love. Our services and programs offer something for everyone, from children to seniors, and we are always eager to welcome new faces into our family.

Pastor Phil Jackson
